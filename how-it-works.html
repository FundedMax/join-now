What is FundedMax?

FundedMax is an innovative platform designed to help traders with limited capital gain access to funded trading accounts. We provide an affordable path to trading with significant capital, giving talented traders the opportunity to showcase their skills and earn substantial profits.

Our system is built on the premise that trading talent shouldn't be limited by starting capital. By offering competitions with micro fees, we've democratized access to funded trading opportunities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about FundedMax

How much does it cost to join a competition?

Our trading competitions are priced at just $5.99 to enter, making them accessible to traders of all budget levels. This is significantly lower than typical funded account challenges in the industry, which often cost hundreds of dollars.

What trading platforms do you support?

We support industry-standard trading platforms that are widely used by professional traders. Specific platform information is provided when you join a competition.

How are winners determined in competitions?

Winners are determined based on trading performance metrics, including profit percentage, risk management, and trading consistency. All competitions have clear rules and evaluation criteria to ensure fairness.

What markets can I trade with FundedMax?

Our funded accounts typically allow trading in forex, commodities, indices, and other liquid markets. Specific tradable instruments are detailed in each competition description.

How do I receive profits from my funded account?

Profits from funded accounts are distributed according to a profit-sharing agreement. Typically, traders receive a substantial percentage of the profits they generate. Payouts are processed on a regular schedule after successful trading periods.
